Doberman Pinscher

From Fearless War Hero to Loyal Companion

The Doberman Pinscher is a medium-sized breed known for its loyalty and fearless nature. Weighing between 60–100 pounds and standing 24–28 inches tall, these dogs have a striking appearance. Their short, sleek coat comes in black, blue, red, and fawn. With a lifespan of 10–13 years, Dobermans make devoted and protective companions.
